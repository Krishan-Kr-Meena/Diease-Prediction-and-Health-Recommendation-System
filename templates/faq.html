Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below.

What is the purpose of this website?

The purpose of this website is to provide users with tools to predict diseases, receive Ayurvedic medicine recommendations, and access information about nearby treatment centers.

Is the information on this website accurate and reliable?

Yes, the core prediction model has high internal accuracy. However, this tool is not a substitute for professional medical advice. We recommend seeking advice from a qualified healthcare professional.

How accurate is the disease predicting model?

The accuracy of the disease predicting model is generally very high based on the training data. We recommend using the Stacking Ensemble model option for the most reliable results.

What should I do if the model predicts a disease?

If the model predicts that you may have a disease, you must seek the advice of a qualified healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan. The AI results are informational only.

Can I use this model to diagnose myself?

**No**. This model is strictly for informational and educational purposes. It cannot replace a comprehensive medical evaluation, lab tests, or professional clinical judgment.

Is Ayurvedic medicine safe?

Ayurvedic remedies can be safe and effective when used correctly and sourced responsibly. Always consult with a healthcare professional or certified Ayurvedic practitioner before beginning any new treatment.
